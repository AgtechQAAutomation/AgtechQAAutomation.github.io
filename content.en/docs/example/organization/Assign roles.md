## Assign Role

The **Assign Role** feature allows organization administrators to assign or update a user’s role and province after the user has been added to the system. This ensures users have the correct access level and operational scope within the RAITHU platform.

Proper role and province assignment helps maintain secure access, clear responsibilities, and efficient regional management.

---

### Accessing Assign Role

1. Log in to RAITHU with an authorized account.
2. Navigate to **User Management → View Users**.
3. Find the user whose role needs to be assigned or updated.
4. Click **Assign** in the Role column.

An **Assign** panel opens on the right side of the screen.

---

### Assign Role Panel

The Assign panel displays the selected user’s information and provides options to configure their role and province.

#### Role

- Select a role from the dropdown based on the user’s responsibilities.
- Available roles may include:
  - Production Head  
  - Agronomist  
  - Central Auditor  
  - Regional Manager  
  - Supervisor  
  - Fulfillment Manager  
  - Field Technician  
  - Fulfillment Operator  

The selected role defines the user’s permissions and access within the platform.

#### Province

- Select the **Province** the user will be responsible for.
- Province assignment restricts data access to the selected geographic region.

> **Note:** Province options may vary depending on the selected role.

---

### Completing the Assignment

After selecting the **Role** and **Province**, click **Done**.

- The user’s role is updated immediately.
- The updated role appears in the user list.
- The user gains access according to the assigned role and province.

---

### Why Assign Role Is Important

The Assign Role feature helps organizations:
- Enforce role-based access control  
- Define regional responsibilities clearly  
- Prevent unauthorized access  
- Manage distributed teams efficiently  

Subsequent sections describe role definitions and permission levels in detail.

## Create Organization – Sign Up

The **Create Organization** page is the starting point for accessing the RAITHU platform. This page allows new users to register their organization and begin setting up their agricultural operations within RAITHU.

RAITHU is designed to operate around organizations. Every user, farmer, field, and workflow is managed within the context of an organization, ensuring secure access control, structured operations, and clear ownership of data.

---

### Purpose of This Page

This page enables you to:
- Register a new organization on the RAITHU platform  
- Associate your work email with the organization  
- Define the country of operation for regulatory and regional configurations  

Once this step is completed, you will be guided through further setup, including organization configuration, workforce onboarding, and policy settings.

---

### Form Fields

To create an organization, provide the following details:

- **Country**  
  Select the country where your organization primarily operates. This helps RAITHU apply region-specific settings and compliance rules.

- **Organization Name**  
  Enter the official name of your company, cooperative, or agricultural program.

- **Work Email**  
  Provide your official work email address. This email will be used for account verification, login, and future communication.

---

### Next Steps

After filling in the required information, click **Continue** to proceed. By continuing, you agree to RAITHU’s **Terms of Service** and **Privacy Policy**.

Once your organization is created, you will be able to:
- Access the RAITHU dashboard  
- Configure workforce roles and permissions  
- Begin onboarding farmers and fields  

The next sections of this manual will walk you through these steps in detail.
